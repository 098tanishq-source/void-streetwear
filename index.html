Support

Questions
We Got
Answers.

Everything you need to know about orders, shipping, and the VOID universe.

Still have questions? Chat us
When does the next drop happen?
add
All drops are announced exclusively on our Instagram @void0 and via WhatsApp to our notify list. Chapter 02 of Urban Monolith drops August 2026. Limited to 50 units — no restocks, ever.
What's your return policy?
add
Returns accepted within 14 days of delivery, original tags attached and unworn. Drop us a WhatsApp message and we'll sort it out directly. Sale items and limited-edition drops are final sale.
How long does shipping take?
add
Pan India delivery in 2–5 business days. Orders placed before 2PM IST are dispatched same day. Free shipping on all orders above ₹2,000. Tracking link sent via WhatsApp once shipped.
How do I know my size?
add
All VOID pieces are intentionally oversized. We recommend sizing down if you prefer a more structured fit. Check our size guide on each product for exact chest/waist/length measurements. When in doubt, WhatsApp us your measurements and we'll guide you.
Can I track my order?
add
Yes. Once dispatched, you'll receive a tracking link via WhatsApp. You can also DM us on Instagram with your order ID and we'll update you instantly.
Are the pieces really limited?
add
Yes. Every drop is a one-time production run. When stock hits zero, it's gone permanently. No restocks, no re-runs. That's the VOID guarantee — exclusivity isn't a marketing trick, it's the design.
